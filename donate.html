Saint Andrews Outdoor Recreation Trail Inc. (SORTI) is a volunteer organization dedicated to building, maintaining and improving the non motorized trail system in Saint Andrews by the Sea, New Brunswick. As a not for profit community focused group, SORTI relies on grants from government, private foundations and donations from individuals to fund maintainence and improvements to the trail system.

If you are interested in making a donation, please contact SORTI so we can organize a charitable receipt.
