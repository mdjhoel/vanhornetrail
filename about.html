ABOUT

Saint Andrews Outdoor Recreation Trail Inc. (SORTI) is a volunteer organization dedicated to building, maintaining and improving the non motorized trail system in Saint Andrews, New Brunswick.

Chairperson of SORTI: David Johnson - sortitrails@gmail.com
